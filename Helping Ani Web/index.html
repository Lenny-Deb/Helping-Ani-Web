HELPING ANI is an psychological-horror game, where you play as Hajime, a boy who has to take something to his girlfriend, Ani. But be careful, the forest will be full of traps and strange creatures!

Talk to the "people" you'll find in the vast forest, solve puzzles and know the real history...

With some hand-drawn drawings, Helping Ani tells a sad and raw history, with dynamic easter eggs and jumpscares, so don't let your guard down!


There are descriptions of suicide, self-harming and blood, so it won't be suitable for all audiences.

This game also contain flashing lights and strong themes, play at your own risk.
